Grizzly Bear

Grizzly bears are very fast even the fastest human athlete can't outrun them....

  • Scientific Name:Ursus arctos horribilis
  • Speed : 35mph(Maximum,Running)
  • Lifespan: 20 to 25 years (In the Wild)
  • Habitat: Grizzlies are highly adaptable and flourish in many places.

Grizzly bears are also great climbers and swimmers.
